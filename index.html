Technology in Education

Transforming how the world learns

Introduction

Technology is revolutionizing education, making it more accessible, personalized, and engaging.

  • Personalized learning paths
  • Flexible schedules and remote access
  • Interactive and multimedia content

Common Tools

  • Google Classroom
  • Zoom & Microsoft Teams
  • Kahoot & Quizizz
  • Smart Boards and AI Tutors

Challenges
